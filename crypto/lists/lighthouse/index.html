Lighthouse

You scan the black night with a lantern, you search all your life

A lighthouse can symbolize various things such as overcoming difficulties, overcoming adversity or guidance. It is most commonly used to symbolize a way forward and to help navigate the world. The lighthouse icon can be divided into two concepts. The first is the lighthouse itself, which can symbolize salvation and security, especially in the face of adversity. The second concept is the lighthouse keeper, the person looking after the lighthouse. Common symbols associated with this concept include a figure who lives alone but always stays awake. This symbol can also mean a person who is awake under the most difficult conditions. 04.26.2022
